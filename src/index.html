What is Beach Bandits?

Beach Bandits makes it easy to plan out a trip between multiple beaches in Florida.

Whether you are running a food truck, helping the community by cleaning up trash, or simply an avid beach lover - Beach Bandits will help you navigate your next multi-beach outing!

Instructions:

Use the drop-down list below to select your starting beach and then click the submit button. An optimal route will be created that will take you to the nearest 10 beaches (starting with the one you've selected) in the shortest distance possible.
